Recipe for Borrito:

1.take tortilla

2.Take your rice and season it with your spices of choice

3.prepare your black beans to add onto your tortilla

4.Add your meat and veggies of choice

5.make your salsa to add onto your tortilla

6.sprinkle some cheese to give it a cheesy flavour

7. roll the burrito up

8.now wrap the burrito up in foil

9.now the burrito is finished
